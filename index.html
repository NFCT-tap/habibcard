ABOUT US

Jawed Habib is one of the most recognizable names in the Indian hair and beauty industry, with a legacy spanning over three decades. Jawed Habib himself comes from a family with a history in hairstyling, but he took the craft to a whole new level by building one of India's largest and most trusted salon chains. Known for combining traditional expertise with modern trends, the brand has expanded to hundreds of outlets across India, offering consistent, high-quality services at affordable prices.

Habib salons provide a full range of services, including haircuts, coloring, hairstyling, skincare treatments, nail art, and even bridal makeup. With an emphasis on current trends and techniques, stylists are trained to cater to diverse beauty needs, whether simple cuts or complex treatments.
